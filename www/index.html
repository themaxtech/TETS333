Demo: Push Notification via GCM

Simulated Room Temperature Controller

Android Instruction:

  1. Install this apk on your Android device
  2. Once your device is registered to this demo, you will get your 8-digit unique ID

Desktop Instruction:

  1. Enter your 8-digit ID below
  2. Hover cursor over the temperature controller UI, and use mousewheel or trackpad to change the value
  3. When you set the room temperature above 80F, it sends you a push notification to your Android device
